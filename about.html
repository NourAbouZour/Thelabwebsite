Our Heritage

Naggiar stands at the forefront of precision metal fabrication, combining traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. For decades, we have been the trusted partner for architects, designers, and artists who demand excellence in metalwork.

Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us the premier choice for Lebanon's most ambitious design projects, from intimate art pieces to monumental installations that define spaces and inspire communities.

Innovation in Metal

Our state-of-the-art facilities feature advanced laser cutting, precision welding, and custom finishing capabilities. We transform raw materials into architectural masterpieces, from intricate sculptures to large-scale installations.

Every project benefits from our decades of expertise in metalworking, where artistry meets engineering. Our team of skilled craftspeople and technicians work hand-in-hand to ensure that every detail meets the highest standards of quality and precision.

Collaboration with We Design

This partnership represents the perfect fusion of artistic vision and technical precision. Together, we push the boundaries of what's possible in contemporary design, creating pieces that are both functional and breathtakingly beautiful.

The Artist Cartel collection showcases eleven exceptional Lebanese and international artists, each bringing their unique vision to life through Naggiar's masterful fabrication. From sculptural furniture to kinetic lighting, each piece is a testament to the power of collaboration between artistic expression and technical excellence.

Our Process

We begin with the artist's vision and translate it into reality through a meticulous process that includes 3D modeling, material selection, precision cutting, expert welding, and custom finishing. Each step is carefully monitored to ensure the final piece exceeds expectations.

Our capabilities include laser cutting up to 10mm steel, CNC milling for custom components, specialized welding techniques including micro-welding for delicate work, and a full range of finishing options from mirror polish to powder coating and oxidation treatments.
